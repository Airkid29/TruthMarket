How the model works

50%
ELO Rating
Gold-standard competitive rating system. Each team's ELO is normalised across the full field to produce valid win probabilities — the same method used by FIFA and the chess world.
30%
Historical success
Titles and finals appearances weighted with Laplace smoothing to avoid zero-probability teams. Past champions carry structural advantages: federation depth, player pipelines, big-game experience.
20%
Recent tournament form
Last tournament result scored on a 10-point scale and normalised. Captures current squad momentum and coaching quality beyond what historical ELO alone can express.
